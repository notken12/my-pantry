About Us

Inspired by the pantry at our school that helps with storing and distributing necessities to help support low-income families, the MyPantry app's functions benefit organizers and users.

Pantry Managers can:

    Start and organize pantries easily
    Easily publicize pantries through links
    More easily provide for their communities by having MyPantry do the bookkeeping

Users can:

    Find pantries if in need of support
    Be kept up to date on what items are available
